Sela Pass

Entry fee:No fees

Weather: -2 to 7 degree celcius

Timimgs:Day Time

It is a high-altitude mountain pass located on the border between the Tawang and West Kameng districts.It has an elevation of 4170 m (13,700 ft) and connects the Indian Buddhist town of Tawang to Dirang and Guwahati

Sela Lake, near the summit of the pass, is one of approximately 101 lakes in the area that are sacred in Tibetan Buddhism.

Sela Pass is breathtaking place always covered in snow providing some beautiful views of the eastern Himalayan range

Can be reach via roadways from route number 13 from Tawang. You can get plenty of taxis to get here.
