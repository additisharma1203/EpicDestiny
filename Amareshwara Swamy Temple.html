Amareshwara Swamy Temple

Diety:Lord Shiva

Timings:6A.M to 12P.M and 4:00P.M to 8:00P.M

Festivals:Maha Shivarahtri,Kartika Purima

Fees:Free

Best time to visit: January to March

Visit Duration:1-2 hours

Situated at banks of Krishna river.

Shivalinga in this temple has been installed by Lord Indra.

Shiv Linga has a red stain on it, as the saying goes Shivalinga was getting bigger, so a nail was driven at the top of it,it looks like the nail went thorugh the Shivlinga,blood started coming out from it.

The holy place embraces a 15 ft high marble Shiv Ling. It is believed that Lord Shiva is present in the structure of five lingams - Pranaveswara, Agasteswara, Kosaleswara, Someswara and Parthiveswara.
