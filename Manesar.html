Manesar

Weather : 8 - 22°C

Tags : Stay Experience

Timings : Anytime

Entry Fee : No entry fee

Manesar is a unique combination of modern and traditional lifestyles and is an upcoming destination that needs to be visited on your next road trip from Delhi to Jaipur. It is highly advised to you spent at least a day in this town to experience its farm tourism, its spas and relax, before you head out on a long journey ahead.

Manesar is a developing industrial suburb and is fast turning into a preferred destination to experience the rugged rural life. The place is famous for its rustic life and the majestic views of the wonderful Aravali Hills offered by it. Moreover, Manesar's farm tourism is considered to be a must-stop on the journey towards Jaipur.

There are a few resorts in Manesar which offer a rural Haryanvi experience. Based on a concept similar to that of Choki Dhani of Jaipur, these resorts offer different activities for an entire day and campfires by the night. Overall, Manesar gives the people of Delhi an interesting rural offbeat experience and a good change from the usual city life.

Shopping At Manesar:
Manesar and its neighbouring cities are quite famous for shopping in Gurgaon. There are local markets and malls in and around Manesar that people flock for essentials frequently. The malls, obviously, are on the pricier side but have top end international brands whereas the local stuff is budget friendly. Kashan Road Market, Krishna Complex, Bhumi Market, Bhagwati Complex, Shopping Complex Sector 1, Rampura Chowk Market are some of the local markets. Sapphire Mall, Raheja Mall, Unitech Arcadia etc. are some of the famous malls in the region which are located little distance away from Manesar.

Best Time To Visit Manesar:
Manesar, Gurgaon is best visited between the months of November and February which is wintertime in the region. The temperature is pleasant, between 28°C and 7°C. January and December are the coldest months. People may also visit Manesar during the spring season, which begins by the end of February and lasts for the entire month of March. The temperature hovers between 24°C and 30°C, which is quite pleasant to explore the region
